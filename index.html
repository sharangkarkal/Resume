A
Portable. The structure is brief → asset → variant testing, with AI accelerating the asset and variant steps. Works for any brand with a creative-heavy funnel — apparel, food, fintech, EdTech, anything with paid distribution.

Q
How do you decide which IP partnerships are worth the spend?
A
Audience overlap first, fit second, reach third. Most brands flip those. The cheapest sponsorship is the one your customer actually cares about; the most expensive is the one that looks good in a press release.
